Find Your Discord Username

Save the username other players should add when your request is approved.

1

Open Discord and click your profile area in the bottom-left corner or open User Settings.

2

Go to My Account and look for the username shown on your profile.

3

If Discord shows a modern username, save that exact name. If your account still uses a tag, save it in the format username#1234.

4

If you need help finding it visually, use the wikiHow guide linked above, then paste your username into this field.
